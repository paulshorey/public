BETA Tester Instructions

Thank you very much for participating in BETA testing of Spiral’s new banking app. We truly appreciate your help in making this app more intuitive and user friendly.

Since this app is currently in private beta and isn’t available to the public yet, there are several steps involved in downloading it onto your phone. Apple facilitates beta testing via their proprietary app called TestFlight. You will need to download it first and then download the Spiral app from TestFlight. No worries, it’s a simple process and it will only take a couple of minutes. Follow the instructions in this document and you shouldn’t have any issues. If you run into any problems please email support@spiral.us.

Step 1 - Invite Email

You should receive an email from Apple inviting you to test Spiral - Mobile Banking app.

Open it on your iPhone (not a desktop or a laptop) and click View in TestFlight.

Step 2 - Download TestFlight

You will be presented with an invitation to test Spiral’s app. Before proceeding, please copy the redeem code you see on the invitation screen under step 3.

Then click Get TestFlight from the App Store link. You will be redirected to the App Store where you can then download TestFlight.

Step 3 - Download Spiral - Mobile Banking app

You should see the Spiral app already inside the TestFlight ready to be installed.

If you don’t click the Redeem link in the upper right corner and enter the redeem code you copied previously. Spiral app will be downloaded to your phone.

That’s it! Welcome to the world of ethical banking.

Once you download and open the Spiral app you will be presented with the following screens. Click Start Testing and then Next to proceed into the app.

Now that you have successfully downloaded Spiral Banking App we ask that you please perform the following 4 actions. As always if you run into any issues don’t hesitate to contact support at support@spiral.us

Action 1 - Sign Up for the Account

Action 2 - Fund Your Account

Action 3 - Setup Giving and donate $5 to the charity of your choice

Action 4 - Open Savings Account

View explanations for each action on the next page.

Walk-throughs

Action 1 - Sign Up for the Account

On the first screen click Join Now and follow the instructions on setting up your account. We will ask you for your email and to create a password as well as to provide your legal name, address, phone number, and social security number. You will be able to review and edit all the information before submitting it. If we need any supporting documentation to approve your application we will let you know. If you run into any issues please contact support at support@spiral.us.

Action 2 - Fund Your Account

On your home screen you should see a card prompting you to fund your account. Click it and follow the instructions to connect your other bank account to Spiral and transfer any amount you desire into your Spiral checking account.

Spiral uses Plaid to connect to your other financial institution; Plaid will ask you to select your bank and then enter the other bank’s credentials in order to link it to Spiral.

Action 3 - Setup Giving and donate to the charity of your choice.

On your home screen you should see a card prompting you to set up your Giving Account. Follow the instructions.

You will need to select what percentage of every deposit you would like to allocate to giving.

Once the Giving Account is established you should see $10 deposited into it as a thank you from Spiral for being our beta tester. You can choose to donate it to the charity of your choice. Please select a charity from the list and donate whatever amount you’d like.

Action 4 - Open Savings Account

On your home screen you should see a card prompting you to open a Savings Account. Click it and follow the instructions.

We will give you an option to set up a goal for your Savings account and an auto transfer if you choose to do so.
